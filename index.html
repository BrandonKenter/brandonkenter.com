About Me

Hello! My name is Brandon Kenter and I am a software engineer living in Madison, Wisconsin. I graduated from the University of Wisconsin-Madison in 2022 with a bachelor's degree in computer science.

Since graduating, I have been working on leveling up my skills and working on projects. Recently I have been working on my flagship project, BluThumb.io. BluThumb.io is a learning tool for people studying technical interview questions by using features that facilitate learning with spaced repetition. It features a list of 300 high-value LeetCode problems, curated by me, after solving over 1000 problems. To learn more about BluThumb.io or my other projects, please go to the Projects tab.
